Our Story

Rooted in Kenya, reaching the world.

Safari Tours was born from a simple idea: that the life-changing experience of travel should be within everyone's reach. Founded in the heart of Kenya, with the majestic savanna as our backyard, we witnessed the profound impact that exploring new cultures and landscapes has on the human spirit.

However, we also saw that for many, the financial barrier to such adventures was too high. That's why we created our unique "Save Up Slowly" model. It's not just a payment plan; it's a partnership. We work with you to plan your dream trip far in advance, allowing you to contribute towards it at a pace that suits your budget.

Our roots are proudly Kenyan, and we carry the spirit of "Harambee" – coming together – in everything we do. We are committed to responsible tourism, supporting local economies, and offering special pricing for locals to explore their own beautiful countries. From our home base, we've expanded our horizons to offer curated tours across the globe, each one designed to be authentic, immersive, and unforgettable.

Join us, and let's build your next adventure, together.
